# Sample Headlines — True or False News (Game 1)

Create one HeadlineData ScriptableObject per row below.
Right-click in Unity Project > Create > BrainCitizen > Headline

---

## REAL Headlines

| # | Headline | Category | Source Hint | Explanation |
|---|----------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | The United Nations has 193 member states as of 2024. | Politics | Source: UN.org | The UN currently recognizes 193 member states. Two observer states (Vatican City and Palestine) are not full members. |
| 2 | Vaccines have eradicated smallpox globally. | Health | Source: WHO, 1980 | Smallpox was officially declared eradicated in 1980 — the first disease eliminated by vaccination. |
| 3 | The Internet was invented in the 1960s as a US military project. | Science | Source: DARPA history | ARPANET, the predecessor to the Internet, was funded by the US Department of Defense in 1969. |
| 4 | Antarctica is a continent covered by a thick ice sheet. | Science | Source: National Geographic | Antarctica holds about 90% of the world's ice and 70% of its fresh water. |
| 5 | Every country in the world has signed the UN Convention on the Rights of the Child. | Politics | Source: UNICEF | The UNCRC is the most widely ratified human rights treaty — 196 countries are party to it. |
| 6 | Climate change is causing the Arctic to warm faster than the rest of Earth. | Science | Source: NASA / IPCC | Arctic amplification means the Arctic warms 2–4× faster than the global average. |

## FAKE Headlines

| # | Headline | Category | Source Hint | Explanation |
|---|----------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 7 | Scientists have confirmed that humans only use 10% of their brains. | Science | No credible source found | This is a popular myth. Brain scans show that virtually all parts of the brain are active throughout the day. |
| 8 | Drinking eight glasses of water a day is required by law in most countries. | Health | No credible source found | The "8 glasses a day" rule is a myth — hydration needs vary per person. No country mandates water intake. |
| 9 | The United Nations was founded in 1900 to prevent World War I. | Politics | No credible source found | The UN was founded in 1945, after World War II, not in 1900. The League of Nations (1920) preceded it. |
| 10 | A single social media post can legally override a government election result. | Politics | No credible source found | Social media posts have no legal power over election results. Election laws are enforced by governments and courts. |
| 11 | Scientists discovered that eating chocolate daily cures the common cold. | Health | No credible source found | No scientific evidence supports chocolate as a cold cure. This is health misinformation. |
| 12 | The Great Wall of China is visible from space with the naked eye. | Science | No credible source found | Multiple astronauts have confirmed the Great Wall is too narrow to be seen from orbit without aid. |
