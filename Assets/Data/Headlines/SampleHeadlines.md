# Sample Headlines — True or False News (Game 1)

Create one HeadlineData ScriptableObject per row below.
Right-click in Unity Project > Create > BrainCitizen > Headline

Pool target: ~30 headlines (15 real + 15 fake) so a 10-question round has real variety.

---

## REAL Headlines

| # | Headline | Category | Source Hint | Explanation |
|---|----------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | The United Nations has 193 member states as of 2024. | Politics | Source: UN.org | The UN currently recognizes 193 member states. Two observer states (Vatican City and Palestine) are not full members. |
| 2 | Vaccines have eradicated smallpox globally. | Health | Source: WHO, 1980 | Smallpox was officially declared eradicated in 1980 — the first disease eliminated by vaccination. |
| 3 | The Internet was invented in the 1960s as a US military project. | Science | Source: DARPA history | ARPANET, the predecessor to the Internet, was funded by the US Department of Defense in 1969. |
| 4 | Antarctica is a continent covered by a thick ice sheet. | Science | Source: National Geographic | Antarctica holds about 90% of the world's ice and 70% of its fresh water. |
| 5 | Every country in the world except the US has signed the UN Convention on the Rights of the Child. | Politics | Source: UNICEF | The UNCRC is the most widely ratified human rights treaty — 196 countries are party to it; the US has signed but not ratified. |
| 6 | Climate change is causing the Arctic to warm faster than the rest of Earth. | Science | Source: NASA / IPCC | Arctic amplification means the Arctic warms 2–4× faster than the global average. |
| 7 | The European Union has 27 member states after Brexit. | Politics | Source: europa.eu | The UK formally left the EU in January 2020, leaving 27 members. |
| 8 | Handwashing with soap can reduce diarrheal disease by nearly 40%. | Health | Source: WHO / CDC | Handwashing is one of the most cost-effective public health interventions. |
| 9 | The Universal Declaration of Human Rights was adopted in 1948. | Politics | Source: UN.org | The UN General Assembly adopted the UDHR on December 10, 1948 — celebrated as Human Rights Day. |
| 10 | The ozone layer over Antarctica has been gradually recovering since the Montreal Protocol. | WorldEvents | Source: UNEP, 2023 | The 1987 Montreal Protocol phased out ozone-depleting substances; recovery is on track for mid-century. |
| 11 | Women won the right to vote in Saudi Arabia in 2015. | WorldEvents | Source: BBC News, 2015 | Saudi women voted and stood as candidates in municipal elections for the first time in December 2015. |
| 12 | Regular physical exercise can reduce symptoms of depression. | Health | Source: WHO mental-health guidelines | Exercise is recommended as a first-line treatment for mild-to-moderate depression alongside therapy. |
| 13 | The Berlin Wall fell in November 1989. | WorldEvents | Source: history.com | East Germany opened its borders on November 9, 1989, leading to the wall's dismantling. |
| 14 | Only three countries have not adopted the metric system as their official measurement. | Science | Source: NIST | The US, Liberia, and Myanmar are the only countries that have not officially adopted the metric system. |
| 15 | Voter turnout in many democracies is higher in elections held on weekends or public holidays. | Politics | Source: IDEA, comparative studies | Studies show weekend voting and holiday voting tend to increase turnout by reducing scheduling barriers. |

| 30 | The Sahara Desert has always been a desert for all of human history. | Science | No credible source found | The "Green Sahara" period (~11,000–5,000 years ago) saw the Sahara as grassland with lakes and rivers. |
